Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

CeliacSaver collects information you provide directly to us, such as:

  • Receipt images and data you upload for analysis
  • Product preferences and dietary restrictions
  • Email address for account creation (optional)
  • Usage data to improve our service

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Analyze your receipts and provide gluten-free alternative recommendations
  • Improve our product matching algorithms
  • Provide customer support
  • Send you updates about new features (with your consent)

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. Receipt images are processed securely and can be deleted at any time from your account settings.

4. Third-Party Services

CeliacSaver may use third-party services for OCR processing, product data, and analytics. We ensure these partners maintain appropriate privacy protections.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of communications

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@celiacsaver.com
